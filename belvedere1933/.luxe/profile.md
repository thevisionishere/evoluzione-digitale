# Business Profile — Ristorante Belvedere dal 1933

## Value Type
**Experience/Atmosphere** — This is a fine dining restaurant on the Frascati hilltop where the panoramic view, the historic ambiance, and the carefully crafted Mediterranean cuisine all combine into a holistic sensory experience. Customers don't just eat here — they live an experience. The name itself ("Belvedere") means "beautiful view."

## Primary Conversion Lever
Primary: **Location/Convenience** — The panoramic hilltop location overlooking Rome and the Castelli Romani is THE unique draw. No competitor can replicate this view. The site must immerse visitors in this atmosphere immediately.
Secondary: **Credentials/Trust** — Since 1933, "Attività Storica del Lazio" certified. A near-century of history provides deep credibility. Two chef brothers carrying on a family legacy reinforces trust.

## Offering Complexity
Level: **Simple** (3-4 services)
Count: 4 services (Ristorante, Catering, Menu Digitale, Eventi Privati)
Categories: No
Public Prices: No (menu consultabile via link esterno leggimenu.it)

## Top 3 Differentiators
1. **History/Legacy** — Founded in 1933, certified "Attività Storica del Lazio" by the regional government. 90+ years of continuous service in the same location. Transformed from trattoria to fine dining in 1998.
2. **Location/Panorama** — Perched on the Frascati hilltop with a panoramic terrace overlooking Rome. The name "Belvedere" literally celebrates this. No competitor has this view.
3. **Family/Team** — Brothers Alain and Nelson Rosica lead the kitchen together. A "quattro mani" (four hands) approach to Mediterranean cuisine with km zero ingredients. Personal, family-driven business.

## Customer Profile
Primary: **Impulse/Emotional** — Visitors are drawn by atmosphere, food photography, the promise of a special evening. They scroll Instagram, see beautiful food, check the website for vibes before booking.
Secondary: **Quick Info Seeker** — Once sold on the atmosphere, they need hours, phone number, booking options quickly.
First thing they need: Immersive visual experience (the view, the food, the atmosphere) + easy way to book/call.

## Competitive Positioning
Position: **Heritage Premium** — A blend of Premium/Luxury and Heritage/Traditional. This is NOT a trendy new spot — it's a 90-year institution that has evolved into fine dining. The design must communicate timeless elegance with warmth. Refined but not cold. Historic but not dated.
Design personality: Warm, golden, intimate. Dark sections with warm lighting feel. Generous whitespace. Classic serif typography for heritage gravitas. Earth tones and gold accents that feel Mediterranean. Slow, dignified animations. Everything should feel like a candlelit dinner with a panoramic view.

## Surprise Element
Chosen: **"Dal 1933 ad Oggi — La Nostra Storia"** — A scroll-triggered animated timeline showing the restaurant's evolution: 1933 founding as trattoria → 1998 Rosica family transformation → Today's innovative Mediterranean cuisine. This is business-specific (no template has a 90-year timeline), emotionally resonant (heritage story), and leverages their strongest differentiator.

## Design Variant Selections
Preloader: Variant **A** — Logo + Line — The "BELVEDERE" Art Deco logo is iconic and deserves the spotlight. Gold line expanding under the name = elegance + heritage.
Hero: Variant **A** — Centered Fullscreen — Premium experience business needs immersive fullscreen atmosphere. Dark overlay on panoramic/food image, centered text, stats at bottom.
Palette: Archetype **C** — Warm Earth (customized) — Their existing gold #dc902e is a warm amber tone that fits perfectly in the Warm Earth family. Customized with their specific brand colors: dark warm browns, cream lights, gold accent. Mediterranean warmth without cold corporate blue.
Font Combo: **3** — Warm Editorial — Libre Baskerville (display) + Source Sans 3 (body) + Cormorant Italic (accent). Perfect for food/storytelling: the serif display has editorial gravitas, the body sans is clean and readable, the Cormorant italic adds elegance for accent text and quotes. Warm and inviting.
Header: Style **C** — Centered Logo — The "BELVEDERE" logo is the brand centerpiece. Centered layout with nav split left/right feels editorial and boutique. Matches the heritage premium positioning.
Mobile Menu: Style **A** — Fullscreen Overlay — Classic, dark, elegant. Large stacked links, stagger reveal. Fits the premium feel.
About Layout: **C** — Timeline — The 1933→1998→Today history arc is the core differentiator. A vertical timeline showcases this perfectly, with each milestone having its own moment.
CTA Banner Style: **B** — Image Background — Use the panoramic terrace view as background with dark overlay. "Prenota la tua esperienza" over the view that awaits them.

## Architecture Implications
- Single-page site: all content on Home, sections must be rich and varied
- Hero MUST open with atmospheric/panoramic imagery — this is the #1 draw
- Timeline/History section gets prominent placement (early in page) — strongest differentiator
- Team section must be personal and warm (large format, not small grid) — only 2 chefs
- CTA strategy: phone call + WhatsApp as primary actions (restaurant booking is phone-based)
- Gallery uses both scraped images and Unsplash supplements for panoramic/food shots
- Reviews must be created (none from research) — keep them specific and credible
- FAQ needed for practical info: hours, parking, reservations, dietary needs
- Map is essential (location IS the product)
