Getting Here

How to Find Us

Located on Dragonara Road in the heart of Paceville, St Julian's. We sit close to the iconic Dragonara Casino and the Westin Dragonara Resort, making us easy to spot on Malta's most vibrant entertainment strip.

Easily accessible by bus (routes serving St Julian's stop just nearby), taxi, or ride-share services. Limited street parking is available along Dragonara Road for those arriving by car.

Look for us right on the main Dragonara Road strip — we're just steps from Paceville's central entertainment area. Follow the warm golden glow and the sound of good music.
