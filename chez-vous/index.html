Our Story

Where Every Evening Becomes an Experience

Chez Vous 3.0 was born from a vision to redefine Paceville's nightlife. We believe every evening deserves to be extraordinary — a carefully orchestrated symphony of flavours, ambiance, and connection. Our Mediterranean soul meets a cosmopolitan spirit, creating a space where local warmth and international sophistication coexist effortlessly.

From fresh local ingredients in our kitchen to premium spirits behind our bar, every detail is curated to engage your senses. With live music and DJ sets setting the rhythm, each visit becomes a unique chapter in your story.

"We don't just serve drinks — we craft moments."
